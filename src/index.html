Formula Reference

SGPA = Σ(Ci × Gi) / ΣCi

Where:

  • Ci = Credits of ith course
  • Gi = Grade points obtained in ith course

CGPA = Σ(Si × Ci) / ΣCi

Where:

  • Si = SGPA of ith semester
  • Ci = Total credits in ith semester
